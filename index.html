How It Works

From sensor
to insight

BitStorm bridges the gap between clinical-grade monitoring and everyday wearability — making precision health data accessible to everyone.

1
Clip & Connect

Attach the BitStorm PPG sensor to your earlobe. The app pairs via Bluetooth in seconds.

2
Real-Time Processing

Our signal processing engine reads your photoplethysmography waveform and computes Heart Rate, HRV, and SpO₂ continuously — being updated every second.

3
Smart Insights & Alerts

The dashboard visualises live waveforms and flags anomalies instantly. Your full history is stored securely so you can track trends over time.
